Lesson Plan: Maharana Pratap

Home ML Page 1 ML Page 2 Maharana Pratap

🕒 Lesson Duration: 60 Minutes

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Understand the historical significance of Maharana Pratap in Indian history.
  • Analyze the Battle of Haldighati and his resistance against Mughal expansion.
  • Appreciate values like bravery, independence, and patriotism.
  • Reflect on historical leadership and moral choices.

📚 Lesson Breakdown

Time Activity Resources
0–5 min Introduction & Icebreaker Ask: “Who is your favorite Indian historical hero and why?”
5–15 min Presentation: Life of Maharana Pratap 🎞️ PowerPoint or Google Slides
15–25 min Video Screening: Battle of Haldighati 📺 YouTube video clip
25–35 min Discussion & Q&A What makes him a hero? Why didn’t he surrender?
35–45 min Interactive Quiz 💡 Kahoot / Quizizz
45–50 min Reading & Reflection 📄 Quotes from Maharana Pratap and historians
50–55 min Creative Writing Task ✍️ Diary Entry: "A Day Before the Battle of Haldighati"
55–60 min Conclusion & Homework Summary + creative project

📌 Suggested Homework

  • 🎨 Creative Poster/Collage on themes like courage, honor, resistance
