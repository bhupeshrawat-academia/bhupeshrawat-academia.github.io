Lesson Plan: Introduction to Machine Learning

Home Page 1 Page 2

Objectives

  • Understand basic concepts of ML and its importance.
  • Identify supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning.
  • Explore real-world ML applications.
  • Understand training/testing of models.

Lesson Structure

1. Introduction to Machine Learning (15 mins)

Hook: Ask about YouTube/Netflix recommendations to introduce ML.

Overview: ML is a subset of AI where computers learn from data to make predictions.

Key Concepts:

  • Data
  • Algorithms
  • Models
